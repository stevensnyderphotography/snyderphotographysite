The photographer

About

Steven Snyder

Photography is how I pay attention. Behind a camera, I slow down, look closer, and find the extraordinary inside the ordinary — a flash of color in the forest canopy, a moment of pure joy at a community celebration.

I shoot a wide range of subjects: wildlife (particularly birds), events, and the natural world. Whether I'm tracking a rare species through dense Caribbean rainforest or capturing a community gathered in song, my goal is always the same — to make a photograph that earns a second look.

Based in the United States, I travel for photography whenever the birds and the calendar cooperate.

All images on this site are © Steven Snyder. Please contact me before using any photograph.

— Steve
